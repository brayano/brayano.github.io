Brayan Ortiz

 

I am a fifth year PhD student in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Washington.

In collaboration with Noah Simon, we develop finite approximations to infinite dimensional functional problems. These finite approximations can be applied to a wide variety of problems, which include signal denoising, spatial modeling, and epidemiology.

I also work as a statistical consultant. A group that I work closely with is the Sonora Cancer Research Center. We work on determining the validity of immunotherapy treatments in relapse prevention for highly recurrent tumors.
